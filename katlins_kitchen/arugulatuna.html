Arugula Tuna Salad

3.5 oz can of tuna
2 Tbs apple cider vinegar
1 Tbs olive oil
juice of 1 lemon
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1 Tbs sprouted pumpkin seeds
1 Tbs fat-free feta cheese
1 medium-sized cucumber, diced
4oz arugula
To start, I combine the can of tuna (drained) in a medium size bowl with apple cider vinegar, olive oil, lemon juice, and seasonings.
Then mix it until it is very well combined. I dress the arugula with a little extra oil, vinegar, and salt before I lay it down in a flat dish and top it evenly with the tuna mixture.
After the tuna is evenly distributed atop the arugula, I sprinkle the cucumber, feta, and lastly, pumpkin seed garnish.
